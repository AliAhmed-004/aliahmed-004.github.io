Blocks break into individual sand grains that fall under gravity and settle naturally on the grid.

How to Play

Easy to learn, hard to master

1

Place Blocks

Tap or click on the grid to place the next block. Only one block can be placed at a time, and only when the board is stable.

2

Watch the Sand Fall

The block shatters into sand grains that fall under gravity. They settle into piles and find their way into gaps.

3

Clear Bridges

Once the board stabilizes, any connected bridge of same-colored sand spanning left to right clears away.

4

Chain Combos

When sand above a cleared bridge falls, it may create new bridges for even bigger combo scores.

5

Avoid Game Over

If sand reaches the top 10% of the grid, the game ends. Plan your placements carefully!
